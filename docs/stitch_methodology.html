database Metric Definitions

Metric Formula Description Unit
Unemployment Rate (UR) unemployed / labour force * 100 The percentage of the labour force that is without work and actively seeking it. Example benchmark: 4.10%. %
Labour Force Participation Rate (LFPR) labour force / population (15+) * 100 The proportion of the population aged 15 and above that is either working or seeking work. Example benchmark: 40.20%. %
Worker Population Ratio (WPR) employed / population (15+) * 100 The percentage of the population aged 15 and above that is currently employed. Example benchmark: 38.50%. %
